Data Processing & Storage

At Social Media Templates, we take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your data:

  • All template customization and image processing is done entirely in your browser
  • We do not store any of your uploaded images or customized content
  • No data is sent to our servers during the template creation process
  • Your creations remain private and are only stored locally while you work on them

How It Works

When you use our template customization tools:

  1. All processing happens directly in your web browser
  2. Images and customizations are handled using client-side JavaScript
  3. Downloaded templates are created locally on your device
  4. Your data is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab
For the best experience, we recommend using a modern web browser with JavaScript enabled.
